# OneTap — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: [FILL IN DATE — e.g. June 16, 2026]**

OneTap ("the app", "we", "us") is a trading-card price-checker for iPhone. This policy
explains, in plain language, what information the app does and does not handle. We built
OneTap to need as little of your data as possible.

**Short version: OneTap does not collect personal information, has no user accounts, shows
no ads, and uses no third-party trackers or analytics.**

---

## 1. Information we do NOT collect
We do not collect, store, or sell personal information. Specifically, OneTap has:
- **No accounts or sign-in.** We never ask for your name, email, phone number, or password.
- **No advertising and no ad networks.**
- **No analytics or tracking SDKs**, no advertising identifiers (IDFA), and no cross-app or
  cross-site tracking.
- **No third-party software development kits** that collect data.

## 2. Information processed to provide the service
To show you live listings, the app needs to perform a search:
- **Your search text** (for example, a card or player name) is sent over an encrypted (HTTPS)
  connection to our server, which queries eBay's public listing API and returns the results.
- We use your search text **only** to fulfill that request and return results. We do **not**
  store it after the request is served, do **not** associate it with your identity, and do
  **not** use it for advertising or tracking.
- We do not transmit any device identifier, account, or contact information with the search —
  there is none to send.

## 3. Information stored only on your device
The app keeps a few preferences **locally on your iPhone**. These never leave your device and
are not sent to us:
- Your **recent searches** (so you can re-run them). You can clear these any time with the
  "Clear" button on the home screen.
- Your selected **marketplace/currency** preference.

Removing the app from your device deletes this local data.

## 4. Camera and photos
This version of OneTap does **not** use your camera or photo library and does not request
access to them.

## 5. Links to eBay
OneTap shows listings from eBay and lets you tap through to view a listing or sold prices on
eBay's website or app. When you do, you leave OneTap and eBay's own privacy practices apply.
We are not responsible for eBay's handling of your information. See eBay's privacy policy at
https://www.ebay.com/help/policies/member-behaviour-policies/user-privacy-notice-privacy-policy.

## 6. Service providers
Our server is hosted by a cloud provider (Render) that processes search requests on our behalf
to deliver results. Requests are transient and are not used to build a profile of you.

## 7. Data retention
Because we do not collect personal information, there is nothing personal to retain. Search
requests are processed transiently to return results and are not kept in a way that identifies
you.

## 8. Children's privacy
OneTap is a general-audience app and does not knowingly collect information from children. We
do not collect personal information from anyone.

## 9. Security
Connections between the app and our server use HTTPS encryption. Because we hold no personal
data and no accounts, there is no personal database to breach.

## 10. Your choices
- Clear your recent searches at any time from the home screen.
- Delete the app to remove all locally stored preferences.

## 11. Changes to this policy
If we change how OneTap handles data (for example, if a future version adds new features), we
will update this policy and revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be
reflected here before the new version ships.
