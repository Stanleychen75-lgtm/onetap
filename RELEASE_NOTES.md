# OneTap — Release Notes

## Version 1.0 (first public release)

OneTap is the fastest way to check what a trading card is selling for.

**What's in 1.0**
- Search any card by name or details and see real, live eBay listings.
- Clean, ranked results with photos, prices, and condition — load more to dig deeper.
- Tap any listing photo to zoom in (pinch, pan, double-tap).
- Choose your marketplace/currency (US, UK, Australia, Canada, Germany) — prices shown as listed, with no conversion.

**About sold prices**
v1.0 focuses on search and live listings. OneTap doesn't show in-app sold prices yet — for real recent sales, tap "See sold comps on eBay" on any card. In-app sold data may come in a later version if eBay grants the access it needs. We never show estimated or sample prices as if they were real sales.

**Privacy**
OneTap collects no personal data, requires no account, and uses no third-party trackers or ad SDKs. Your searches stay on your device; the app only sends a search term to our server to fetch live listings.
